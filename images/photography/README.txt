DROP YOUR TRAVEL PHOTOS HERE
============================

Name your photos exactly as listed below and they will
automatically appear in the portfolio gallery:

  yellowstone-1.jpg   (e.g. Grand Prismatic Spring)
  yellowstone-2.jpg   (e.g. Old Faithful / geyser)
  yellowstone-3.jpg   (e.g. Wildlife / bison)

  newyork-2.jpg       (e.g. Times Square)
  newyork-3.jpg       (e.g. Brooklyn Bridge)

OTHER TRAVEL
  travel-1.jpg        (any location)
  travel-2.jpg        (any location)

TIPS
- JPG or PNG both work
- Landscape orientation works best for regular slots
- Portrait (tall) orientation works well for photo-tall slots
- Any resolution is fine — they auto-crop to fill the grid

The gallery uses the onerror fallback to Unsplash placeholder
images until you add your real photos.
